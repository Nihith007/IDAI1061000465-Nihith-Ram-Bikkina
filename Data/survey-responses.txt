SUMMARY OF KEY FINDINGS
================================================================
 
1. 80% of participants feel stressed every single day at work.
2. Deadlines and heavy workloads are the primary stress triggers (90%).
3. Most people use passive coping methods — there is a clear gap for active solutions.
4. 90% are open to using a dedicated wellness space.
5. 10–15 minute sessions are the preferred duration.
6. Comfortable seating + calming music are the most desired features.
7. 80% accept stress tracking if data stays private (on-device).
8. Afternoons are peak stress hours — best time for pod bookings.
9. 90% are willing to use AI-personalised recommendations.
10. Privacy and time are the key design barriers to address.
 
These findings directly shaped all 5 compulsory prototype features:
  → MoodSync AI        (insights from Q7, Q9)
  → Breathe with Me    (insight from Q6)
  → Pod Scheduler      (insight from Q8, Q10)
  → Post-Session Journal (insight from Q3)
  → Stress Tracking Dashboard (insight from Q1, Q7)
